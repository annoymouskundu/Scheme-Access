Scheme Name:Bina Mulya Samajik Suraksha Yojana (BMSSY)
Eligibility Criteria: The scheme applies to every eligible unorganized worker in West Bengal, including landless laborers, with a family income not exceeding Rupees 65,000 per month. There is no income ceiling for construction workers and transport workers. Beneficiaries must be between 18 and 60 years old.
Benefits:The scheme provides various benefits, including a provident fund scheme where the government contributes Rupees 30 per month, financial assistance for health and family welfare, death and disability benefits, education assistance, and free skill development training to enable beneficiaries to engage in alternative economic activities, with specific amounts allocated for each benefit, including Rupees 2 lakh in case of accidental death, Rupees 50,000 in case of natural death, and Rupees 1 lakh in case of disability.
