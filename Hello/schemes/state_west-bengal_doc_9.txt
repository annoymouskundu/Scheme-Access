Scheme Name: West Bengal CEO Voter List
Eligibility Criteria: The West Bengal CEO Voter List is available for all citizens of West Bengal who have a valid Voter ID Card.
Benefits: The West Bengal CEO Voter List enables citizens to check their name in the district-wise voter list with photo and download their Voter ID Card. The updated WB Electoral Rolls with Photo are available in PDF format, allowing citizens to manually search for their name in the CEO West Bengal Voters List 2020.
