Scheme Name: West Bengal Yuvashree Arpan Scheme
Eligibility Criteria:The scheme is designed for unemployed youths in West Bengal, with a focus on promoting entrepreneurship. To be eligible, applicants must be permanent residents of West Bengal, interested in starting their own business, and have innovative ideas. They should also hold a technical degree from a recognized institution or be an ITI pass-out/diploma holder.
Benefits: The scheme provides financial assistance of Rupees 1 lakh to eligible youths to start their own businesses. This assistance aims to promote entrepreneurship, create jobs, and contribute to the state's economic growth. The scheme is expected to benefit around 50,000 youths in West Bengal.
