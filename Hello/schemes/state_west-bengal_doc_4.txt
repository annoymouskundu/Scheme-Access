Scheme Name: West Bengal Aikyashree Minority Scholarship Scheme
Eligibility Criteria: The West Bengal Aikyashree Minority Scholarship Scheme is designed for students from minority communities. To be eligible, candidates must be domiciles of West Bengal, study in a recognized school or institution, secure at least 50% marks or equivalent grade in the previous final examination, and have a total annual family income not exceeding Rs. 2 lakh for pre-matric and post-matric scholarships, and Rs. 2.5 lakh for merit-cum-means scholarships.
Benefits: The West Bengal Aikyashree Minority Scholarship Scheme provides three types of scholarships: pre-matric, post-matric, and merit-cum-means. The pre-matric scholarship is for meritorious students from Class 1 to Class 10, the post-matric scholarship is for meritorious minority students from Class 11 to Ph.D., and the merit-cum-means scholarship is for minority students pursuing technical and professional courses. The scheme aims to support the education of minority students and encourage them to pursue higher education.
