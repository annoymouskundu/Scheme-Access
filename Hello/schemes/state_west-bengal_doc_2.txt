Scheme Name: West Bengal Karma Sathi Prakalpa Scheme
Eligibility Criteria: The scheme is designed for unemployed youths in West Bengal, with a focus on promoting entrepreneurship. To be eligible, applicants must be permanent residents of West Bengal, have completed their 10th or 12th standard, be unemployed, and be in search of a job.
Benefits: The scheme provides soft loans of up to Rs. 2 lakhs to eligible youths to start their own businesses. This assistance aims to promote entrepreneurship, create jobs, and contribute to the state's economic growth. The scheme is expected to benefit around 3 lakh youngsters in the state over the next three years.
