Scheme Name: WB Didi Ke Bolo
Eligibility Criteria: The WB Didi Ke Bolo scheme is open to all citizens of West Bengal who want to register their grievances, complaints, or suggestions directly with the state government and Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee.
Benefits: The WB Didi Ke Bolo scheme provides a platform for citizens to directly communicate their concerns and grievances to the state government and Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee. Citizens can register their complaints online through the official website or by contacting the helpline number 9137091370.
