Scheme Name: West Bengal Bangla Shashya Bima Yojana
Eligibility Criteria: The West Bengal Bangla Shashya Bima Yojana is designed for farmers in the state. All farmers taking loans (granted/applied for) and even those not taking loans can avail of the scheme's insurance benefits.
Benefits: The West Bengal Bangla Shashya Bima Yojana provides crop insurance to farmers for the Kharif season. The scheme offers free crop insurance, with the state government bearing the premium cost. Insurance payments are made in four stages, and the amount is calculated per hectare. The scheme covers crops like aman paddy, aus paddy, jute, and maize.
