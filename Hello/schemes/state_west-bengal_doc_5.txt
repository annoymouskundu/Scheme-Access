Scheme Name: West Bengal Krishak Bandhu Scheme
Eligibility Criteria: The West Bengal Krishak Bandhu Scheme is designed for farmers in the state. To be eligible, candidates must be residents of West Bengal and have a valid landholding certificate.
Benefits: The West Bengal Krishak Bandhu Scheme provides two main benefits: Assured Income and Death Benefit. Under the Assured Income component, farmers with 1 or more acres of landholding are entitled to receive Rs. 5,000 per annum, paid in two equal installments. The Death Benefit component provides a lump sum amount of Rs. 2 lakh to the families of farmers in case of their death, including suicide.
