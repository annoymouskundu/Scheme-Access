Scheme Name: West Bengal Yuvasree Scheme
Eligibility Criteria: The West Bengal Yuvasree Scheme is designed for unemployed youth in the state. To be eligible, candidates must fulfill the conditions of the Yuvasree Scheme, which includes registration with the Employment Bank.
Benefits: The West Bengal Yuvasree Scheme provides financial assistance to unemployed youth in the state. The scheme has a waiting list, and candidates who have been provisionally identified for the waiting list are required to submit online Annexure I and printout of the same along with filled Annexure 2 to the concerned employment exchange office for validation.
